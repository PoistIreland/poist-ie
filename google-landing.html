poist.ie is an AI-powered recruitment platform using automated CEFR language assessment, map-first job matching, and compliance tracking to connect verified Irish speakers with the employers who legally need them.

Technology

Five distinct tech systems
built for Irish language hiring

poist.ie is not a job board with an Irish filter. It is a purpose-built AI platform where every technical layer is designed around the specific challenges of Irish language recruitment.

🎯

Automated CEFR Assessment

Full adaptive assessment across all four skills — reading, listening, writing and speaking. Reading and listening use adaptive MCQ banks. Writing is scored by AI using structured CEFR rubrics. Speaking clips are stored for recruiter review.

AI-POWERED
📋

Language Passport System

Every candidate gets a verified Language Passport showing their CEFR level, skill breakdown, speaking audio sample, and supporting credentials. Employers see exactly what level they're hiring — no ambiguity.

VERIFICATION
🗺️

Map-First Job Matching

Geographic-first search using Leaflet.js and OpenStreetMap. Candidates and employers search across all 32 counties simultaneously filtered by CEFR level, sector and role type. Pins represent real opportunities.

GEO-MATCHING
⚖️

2030 Compliance Tracking

Employer dashboard shows Irish language hiring progress against Official Languages Act obligations. Built-in reporting helps public bodies track and evidence compliance for CPSA and OGP procurement frameworks.

REGTECH
🔊

Irish Language NLP & Speech

Integration with Abair.ie (Trinity College Dublin) for authentic Irish language TTS across all three dialects — Ulster, Connacht and Munster. Supports listening assessment audio generation without pre-recorded clips.

NLP / SPEECH
⚡

AI Writing Evaluation

Candidate writing samples are scored via the Claude API against a structured CEFR rubric — vocabulary range, grammatical accuracy, coherence, register and task completion. Consistent, scalable, auditable.

GENERATIVE AI
How It Works

From Irish speaker to
verified candidate in minutes

1

Take the language assessment

Adaptive CEFR test covering reading, listening, writing and speaking. AI scores all four skills and assigns a verified level from A1 to C2 in under 25 minutes.

2

Language Passport is generated

A verified profile showing CEFR level, skill breakdown, speaking audio clip and supporting credentials. Visible to all employers on the platform.

3

Map-first job matching

The platform surfaces relevant roles across Ireland filtered to the candidate's verified level. Employers can search by CEFR level, county, sector and availability.

4

Direct employer contact

Employers contact candidates directly through the platform. No intermediary, no guesswork — both sides know the language level before the first conversation.
