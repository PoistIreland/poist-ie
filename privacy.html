1. Who we are

poist.ie is operated by Séamus Ó Fátharta, a sole trader trading as poist.ie, based in Ireland. We are Ireland's dedicated Irish language jobs platform, connecting CEFR-verified candidates with employers who need Irish-speaking staff.

Data Controller: Séamus Ó Fátharta trading as poist.ie
Contact: privacy@poist.ie

As data controller, we are responsible for deciding how and why your personal data is processed. We are subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) as implemented in Irish law by the Data Protection Acts 1988–2018, and are registered with the Data Protection Commission (DPC) Ireland.

Note on future incorporation: poist.ie intends to incorporate as a limited company in Ireland. When that occurs, this policy will be updated to reflect the new legal entity as data controller. Registered users will be notified by email.

2. What data we collect

For candidates, we collect:

  • Full name and email address (account creation)
  • County of residence
  • Irish language proficiency level (CEFR — self-declared)
  • Profile type (active / passive job seeker)
  • Skills and job title (optional, self-entered)
  • Short biography (optional, self-entered)
  • Speaking clip audio recording (optional, uploaded to secure storage)
  • Assessment results if you complete the CEFR assessment
  • Date of account creation and last active date

For employers, we collect:

  • Organisation name, type, county and website
  • Contact person name, role and work email address
  • Job listings posted (title, description, requirements, salary range)
  • Date of account creation

Automatically collected data:

  • Authentication session data (managed by Supabase)
  • Basic usage logs (page visits, errors) — no advertising tracking
We do not collect nationality, ethnic origin, religious beliefs, political opinions, health data, financial information, or any special category data under GDPR Article 9.

3. How we use your data

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • To operate the platform — creating and managing your account, enabling you to sign in and use poist.ie features
  • Candidate–employer matching — making candidate profiles discoverable to employers based on CEFR level, county and skills
  • Job listings — displaying employer job postings to candidates
  • Platform communications — sending transactional emails (account verification, password reset)
  • Platform improvement — understanding how the platform is used in aggregate, to improve features
  • Legal compliance — meeting our obligations under Irish and EU law
We never sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Legal basis for processing

Under GDPR, we must have a lawful basis for processing personal data. Our bases are:

  • Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b)) — processing necessary to provide the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) — operating, improving and securing the platform, where this does not override your rights
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) — for optional data such as speaking clips and biography, which you provide voluntarily
  • Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)) — where we are required by law to retain or disclose data

5. Who we share data with

We use a small number of trusted third-party processors to operate poist.ie. All processors are GDPR-compliant and bound by data processing agreements:

  • Supabase Inc. — database, authentication and file storage. Our Supabase project is hosted in West EU (Ireland) — data does not leave the EEA. Supabase Privacy Policy →
  • Vercel Inc. — web hosting and deployment. Vercel Privacy Policy →

We do not share your personal data with any other third parties, except where required by law (e.g. in response to a valid legal request from An Garda Síochána or a court order).

Candidate profiles are visible to registered employers on poist.ie. Your email address is not shared with employers — they see your CEFR level, county, skills, bio and profile type only.

6. Data retention

  • Active accounts — data retained for as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts — personal data permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Speaking clips — deleted immediately when removed from your passport, or within 30 days of account deletion
  • Job listings — retained for 12 months after a listing closes, then anonymised
  • Authentication logs — retained by Supabase for up to 90 days for security purposes

7. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data at any time through your passport settings
  • Right to erasure — delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Right to restrict processing — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — for data processed on the basis of consent (e.g. speaking clips), you can withdraw at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@poist.ie. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission (DPC) at dataprotection.ie.

8. Cookies

poist.ie uses a minimal number of cookies:

  • Authentication cookies — set by Supabase to maintain your login session. These are strictly necessary and cannot be disabled.
  • No advertising or tracking cookies — we do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party advertising or behavioural tracking.

Because we use only strictly necessary cookies, we are not required under the ePrivacy Regulations to obtain cookie consent. If we add any non-essential cookies in future, we will update this policy and implement a consent mechanism.

9. Security

Our security measures include:

  • All data encrypted in transit using TLS (HTTPS)
  • Passwords hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain-text passwords
  • Database access controlled by Row Level Security (RLS) — users can only access their own data
  • Speaking clips stored in a private Supabase storage bucket — not publicly accessible
  • Production system access restricted to authorised personnel only

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to privacy@poist.ie.

10. Children

poist.ie is a professional employment platform intended for users aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child under 16 has created an account, please contact privacy@poist.ie and we will delete the account promptly.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify registered users by email and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of poist.ie after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact us

For any privacy-related questions, data subject requests, or concerns, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@poist.ie

We aim to respond to all privacy enquiries within 5 business days, and to all formal data subject requests within 30 days as required by GDPR.
