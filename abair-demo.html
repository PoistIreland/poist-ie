About Abair.ie: Developed by the Phonetics & Speech Laboratory at Trinity College Dublin since 2008. Covers all three major Irish dialects — Ulster (Donegal), Connacht (Connemara) and Munster (Kerry) — with multiple voices per dialect. Free for non-commercial use. For poist.ie commercial use, contact the ABAIR team at abair.ie/contact to discuss licensing.

For the assessment: Abair voices are suitable for A1–B2 clips. For C1–C2 material where natural prosody matters more, commission recordings from a native speaker. Total cost for a full assessment audio bank: estimate €300–600 for professional voice recordings.
