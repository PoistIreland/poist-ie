01 · How it works / Conas a oibríonn sé

Four tiers, one honest level.

Every candidate on poist.ie falls into one of four tiers based on the evidence behind their CEFR level. The tier is visible to employers, searchable as a filter, and designed so candidates have a clear path to strengthen their passport over time.

Tier 01Self-declared

Candidate picked a level.

Shown to employers but flagged. Useful for building a passive profile — not a shortlist.

— user selection only
Tier 02Assessed

poist.ie assessment passed.

In-app AI-driven assessment covering reading, listening and speaking against CEFR descriptors.

+ poist.ie assessment
Tier 03Documented

Qualification uploaded.

TEG, Leaving Cert, degree, or a CEFR certificate. Auto-mapped to a level, reviewed on request.

+ TEG / LC / degree / CEFR cert
Tier 04Corroborated

Assessed and documented.

Two independent signals point at the same level. The strongest passport on the platform.

+ assessment + document
Why this matters. On Indeed, LinkedIn and IrishJobs, "Irish" is a self-declared keyword — every candidate looks the same. On poist.ie, a Tier 4 B2 speaker and a Tier 1 B2 speaker are different products. Employers filter on tier. Candidates are motivated to climb it.
