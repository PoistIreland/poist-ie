🍀 Our Story

About Poist.ie

Ireland's first dedicated platform connecting Irish-speaking candidates with the employers who need them.

⚖️

The Problem

Ireland's Official Languages (Amendment) Act 2021 requires 20% of all new public sector recruits to be competent in Irish by 31 December 2030. That covers approximately 380,000 employees across 3,500+ public bodies.

Until poist.ie, there was no dedicated platform connecting these employers with the Irish-speaking candidates they urgently need.

🎯

The Solution

Poist.ie is a dedicated Irish language jobs platform — a job board and candidate marketplace using the CEFR framework to precisely match candidates with employers.

"Poist" is the Irish word for "jobs/posts" — immediately understood by Irish speakers and intuitive in English context.

Who we serve

🎓 Candidates
  • ✓ Native Irish speakers from Gaeltacht regions
  • ✓ Fluent second-language speakers
  • ✓ Irish medium school graduates
  • ✓ University graduates with Irish qualifications
  • ✓ Public sector workers seeking career advancement
🏛️ Employers
  • ✓ Government departments and agencies
  • ✓ HSE and health bodies
  • ✓ Local authorities (31 councils)
  • ✓ Education providers and ETBs
  • ✓ Semi-state bodies (RTÉ, Bus Éireann, An Post)
  • ✓ Private sector organisations valuing Irish

Our Vision

Poist.ie becomes the definitive platform for Irish language employment in Ireland — the first place any public body goes when they need to hire an Irish speaker, and the first place any Irish speaker goes when they want a job where their language is valued.

Longer term, as Ireland's 2030 deadline focuses minds and budgets, Poist.ie is positioned to become a full-service Irish language recruitment agency.

Currently in Phase 1 — Free Launch

Poist.ie launched in 2026. During our launch phase, all features are free for both candidates and employers. Our goal is to build a critical mass of listings and registered users before introducing paid tiers.
