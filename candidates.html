CEFR Framework

Every level of Irish is in demand

poist.ie uses the Common European Framework of Reference to match you precisely with roles that need your level of Irish.

A1
Beginner

Basic phrases and introductions. Great for customer-facing roles with Irish greeting requirements.

A2
Elementary

Simple everyday exchanges. Suits roles in retail, hospitality or admin with basic Irish signage.

B1
Intermediate

Handle familiar situations and express opinions. Common requirement in public sector and education.

B2
Upper Intermediate

Fluent in most situations. The most sought-after level in media, communications and Government.

C1
Advanced

Express ideas fluently and spontaneously. Required for Irish language broadcasting and senior policy.

C2
Proficient

Complete mastery. Essential for Gaeltacht-based roles, Irish language media and language officer posts.
